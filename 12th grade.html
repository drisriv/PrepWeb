12th Grade



General Advice

1. If you are starting college prep a little late, think about whether or not you want to take a gap year or go to community college.

2. Talk to your counselor.

3. Start applying for scholarships and financial aid.

4. Reach out to your teachers and counselors for letters of recommendation.

5. Stay focused! Most people have heard of “senioritis.” This is when seniors start to procrastinate and get lazy. Finish out your final year in high school strong!

6. Lastly, have fun and attend the senior events (Prom, Senior Cookout, Awards Night, etc.). You have worked hard these past 4 years. You deserve it!
