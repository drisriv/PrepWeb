Our Mission

As high schoolers ourselves, we know that college preparation can be stressful and demanding. Our mission is to help scholars answer some questions they may have on their mind such as what classes they should take, how to get involved in extracurriculars, and so forth. On our site, students will be able to find various resources catered specifically to their grade level as well as advice from other high schoolers.



How to Navigate PrepWeb

1. Locate the navigation bar

2. Click on which grade applies to you

3. Explore the several resources given to you based on your grade level.
